🤲 Eid Dua
تَقَبَّلَ اللَّهُ مِنَّا وَمِنْكُمْ وَكُلَّ عَامٍ وَأَنْتُمْ بِخَيْرٍ
Translation:

"May Allah accept (good deeds) from us and from you, and may you be well every year."

This is the blessed greeting of the Prophet Muhammad ﷺ on the day of Eid. It is Sunnah to say this to fellow Muslims when you meet them on Eid day.

— Collected by Ibn Hajar al-Asqalani in Fath al-Bari
